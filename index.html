An-Zi Yen 顏安孜

Assistant Professor, Dept. of Computer Science, National Yang Ming Chiao Tung University

Dr. An-Zi Yen is an Assistant Professor in the Department of Computer Science at National Yang Ming Chiao Tung University. Her research interests include natural language processing and information retrieval. Her work has been published in AAAI, EMNLP, WWW, SIGIR, and so on. Dr. Yen's awards and honors include the Honorable Mention of Doctoral Dissertation Award of ACLCLP in 2020 and the distinguished PC member in IJCAI 2023. She served as the web chair of SIGIR 2023, and as PC members of representative conferences including AAAI, IJCAI, CIKM, and EACL.

Welcome to NYCU NLP Lab

    Our major research lies in Natural Language Processing and Information Retrieval. We aim to improve and adapt natural language understanding and generation techniques in various applications. Ongoing research topics include:
  • Question Generation and Answering
    • Knowledge Base Question Answering
    • Multimodal Question Answering
    • Unanswerable Question Correction
    • Natural Language Processing (NLP) for Education
  • NLP for Social Good
    • Disinformation and Misinformation Detection
    • Concern Mining
    • Natural Legal Language Processing
  • Explainable Artificial Intelligence
    • Truthfulness and Interpretability
    • Augmented Language Models
